AS207299 is a network operated by Michael Petö. It is used for R&D, in diverse fields, with both commerical and non-commercial background.

Peering

We utilize an open peering policy.
Peering or transit is possible if you fit the specified ruling we have.

In order to peer with us, you must

  • Have a publicly routable ASN.
  • Have at least one public routable IP address space. (minimal size is /48 for IPv6 and /24 for IPv4)
  • Have a record in PeeringDB for your ASN.
  • Only send us traffic that is destined for us, we will do the same.
  • Be willing to connect to us via Internet eXchange, Tunneling, VPN, ...
  • Be able to respond on at least regular hours (preferably on weekdays) in under a day.
For all peers, we use
  • Max-prefix filters on both IPv4 and IPv6.
  • Filters for Bogon prefixes.
In order to get transit, you must
  • Declare how much bandwidth and traffic you will likely use.
  • Fit the same ruling as peers.
More information about AS207299 can be found on PeeringDB and bgp.he.net.
